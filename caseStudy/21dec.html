Spreading Joy and Warmth This Christmas

On December 21, Navriseup India Care Foundation, in collaboration with Vimal Khanderao Rangnekar Prathisthan and with the unwavering support of its dedicated volunteers, hosted a memorable Christmas celebration at the Support School in Ambica Nagar, Thane.

A Heartfelt Gesture of Giving

The event was a testament to the power of collective kindness and compassion. By bringing together resources and efforts, the foundation distributed Christmas gifts, sweets, and snacks, spreading happiness among children who might not otherwise experience the joy of the season.
