Mission and Vision

Christmas Celebration Case Study

On Christmas Eve, Navriseup India Care Foundation arranged a wonderful Christmas event. It was supported by Bake All Day and Vimal Khanderao Rangnekar Prathisthan for the event. It was held at Police School, Thane and people were welcomed with smiling faces. People did their best to contribute to the beautiful atmosphere and put effort into decorating the space as well as preparing the gifts. The children were given some sweets and were able to engage in fun games and other activities as well. It was not only a celebration of the lives of the recipients but also proved that the power of kindness can bring light into the lives of people. This Christmas event proved that such a non-profit Christmas event can be very successful if well-planned and organized. This is evident in the fact that the partners, volunteers, and the foundation showed everyone that partnership is the key to making great memories.
