Empowering communities and building a brighter future.

NavRiseup India Care Foundation is a non-profit organization focused on uplifting underprivileged communities. We work to provide education, healthcare, and sustainable development opportunities, empowering individuals to rise above their circumstances. With compassion and a sense of responsibility, we collaborate with local communities, volunteers, and partners to deliver impactful programs that bridge the gap of inequality and create a more inclusive society. At NavRiseup, we are committed to inspiring hope, driving change, and building a brighter future for all.

Navriseup India Care Foundation Wins Prestigious Recognition at the Universal Eminence Awards 2024!

Navriseup India Care Foundation is thrilled to announce that we have been awarded the prestigious title of "Prominent and Thriving Non-Governmental Organisation" at the Universal Eminence Awards 2024. This recognition highlights our continued commitment to driving positive social change and making a meaningful impact on communities in need. It stands as a testament to the dedication of our team, partners, and supporters who have worked tirelessly to further our mission. We are deeply honored to receive this award and remain motivated to expand our efforts, empowering more lives and fostering a brighter, more inclusive future.

At Navriseup India Care Foundation, we focus on addressing the critical health and education needs of marginalized communities across India. Our work includes providing healthcare services through mobile clinics, organizing educational workshops, offering after-school programs, and supporting vocational training. We also run awareness campaigns on health issues, offer scholarships for underprivileged students, and build partnerships with local organizations to create sustainable, long-term solutions. Our goal is to empower individuals and families to break the cycle of poverty through accessible healthcare, education, and skill-building opportunities.
