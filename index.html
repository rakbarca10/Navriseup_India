Our Mission

Even in modern day India, rural and semi-rural communities are faced with an acute shortage of accessibility to basic resources. Women and children specifically, are vulnerable to an increased risk of health problems emanating from lack of nutrition and access to good quality healthcare. Such marginal groups continue to face challenges that goes above and beyond just a mere lack of good healthcare. Children from such backgrounds continue to drop out of school at alarming rates due to poor health, monetary constraints and lack of necessary support.

Our Vision

Our vision extends beyond humans to encompass all creatures that share our world, advocating for their welfare and responsible care. We envision vibrant rural areas, self-reliant communities, and sustainable development that benefits generations to come.

Why Donate With Us

Donating to NavRiseup India Care Foundation is an opportunity to make a meaningful and lasting impact on the lives of the less privileged in India. Here are compelling reasons why people should choose to donate to NavRiseup India:

1

Empowering Communities:

NavRiseup is dedicated to empowering marginalized communities across India. Your donation directly contributes to initiatives that uplift individuals and families, providing them with opportunities to thrive.

2

Transparency:

NavRiseup is transparent in its operations and financial management. We provide clear information on how your donations are used, ensuring accountability and trust.

3

Holistic Approach:

Our organization addresses a wide range of critical issues, from education and food security to women's empowerment, health, and rural development. Your donation supports a comprehensive approach to social change.

4

Community Engagement:

We engage with and involve the communities we serve, ensuring that our initiatives are community-driven and responsive to local needs.
